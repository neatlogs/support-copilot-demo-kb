Starter Plan Support SLA

Article ID starter-support-sla · Last updated June 1, 2026

Starter plan customers receive email support with a 24 hour response window. Phone callbacks and senior engineer escalation are available only on Business and Enterprise plans.

If a Starter customer asks for a phone call, acknowledge the urgency without promising a callback. Explain the email SLA and collect troubleshooting details for the support queue.
