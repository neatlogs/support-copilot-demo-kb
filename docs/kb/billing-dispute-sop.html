Resolving a Billing Dispute

Article ID billing-dispute-sop · Last updated April 3, 2026

If you see a charge from ACME that you do not recognize, follow these steps:

  1. Confirm the charge amount and date against your bank statement.
  2. Check whether a household member or shared-account user authorized the purchase.
  3. If the charge is still unrecognized, contact support with your account email and the last four digits of the card charged.

Most disputes are resolved within 2 business days.
