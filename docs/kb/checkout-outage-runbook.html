Checkout Outage Troubleshooting

Article ID checkout-outage-runbook · Last updated April 18, 2026

For checkout outages, collect the affected store URL, recent deploy time, payment provider, and a failing order ID. Share immediate mitigation steps while the support queue handles the ticket.

  • Ask for the checkout or store URL.
  • Ask for one failing order ID or cart ID.
  • Ask for the timestamp of the most recent checkout or payment deployment.
  • Ask which payment provider and webhooks are involved.
