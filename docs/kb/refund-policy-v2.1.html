Refund Policy

Article ID refund-policy-v2.1 · Last updated February 18, 2026

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a refund within 30 days of the original transaction date. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5 business days.

To request a refund, reply to your order confirmation email or contact support@example.com.
